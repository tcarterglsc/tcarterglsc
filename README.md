## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a **Software Engineer** at the **Guyana Lands and Surveys Commission (GL&SC)**, where I build and maintain the digital infrastructure that supports national land administration across Guyana. I work across the full stack in designing React/TypeScript and Vue/Ionic frontends, engineering Flask/Python and Laravel backends, and delivering everything from WordPress sites to government-grade web platforms. I'm passionate about applying technology to solve real-world, civic-scale problems.

Beyond my day job, I'm a founder and multi-hat wearer: I previously served as **CEO & CTO at Taekonda Creative Agency**, where I led web development, brand strategy, and IT consulting. I've also worked as **CTO at Excil Marketing** and as a **Part-time Tutor at the University of Guyana**.

I'm always learning, always building, and always open to collaboration.

---

## 🔭 What I'm Working On

- 🏛️ **NextGen Land Management System** — React/TypeScript frontend for internal land administration at GL&SC
- 🌐 **GLSC Client Portal** — A public-facing Next.js portal for citizens to track and manage land applications
- 🗺️ **GIS & Geospatial Tools** — Migrating from ArcGIS to Leaflet, exploring Google Earth Engine for LULCC analysis
- 🚗 **VRAMS** — A full-stack vehicle and asset management system prototype
- 🔐 **Platform Security** — TOTP-based 2FA, API hardening, and secure authentication flows
